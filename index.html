Privacy Policy

We are transparent about the data we collect and committed to keeping your personal information safe and secure.

Last updated: February 2026 Encrypted & secure Block 13, Karachi

The Block 13 Federal B Area Resident Portal, developed by DeskWorkSolutions, is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a resident.

By registering and using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will never sell or share your data with any unauthorized third party.

Section 01
Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you register and use the app:

  • Full name and CNIC (National Identity Card Number)
  • Phone number and email address
  • House number and residential address (Block 13, Federal B Area)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Vehicle registration information, if applicable
  • Payment receipts and transaction IDs
  • Complaint details and communication history
  • Device information and push notification tokens
Section 02
How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for community management and resident services:

  • Verifying your identity as a resident of Block 13 Federal B Area
  • Processing and tracking maintenance fee payments
  • Managing and responding to your complaints and requests
  • Sending push notifications for announcements, payment reminders, and complaint updates
  • Issuing your digital resident identity card
  • Maintaining community security records
  • Administrative approval and account management by authorized personnel
Section 03
Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data may only be accessed in these limited circumstances:

  • By authorized Block 13 RWA administrators for community management purposes
  • By DeskWorkSolutions technical team solely for app maintenance and support
  • When required by law or lawful authority in Pakistan

We will never share your CNIC or payment details with any unauthorized third party.

Section 04
Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS encrypted connections
  • Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized administrators only
  • Authentication tokens expire automatically for your protection

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your credentials confidential.

Section 05
Device Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera & Photo Library — to upload your profile picture and payment receipts
  • Push Notifications — to send community announcements and updates
  • Internet Access — required for all app functionality

You may revoke any permission at any time through your device Settings. Revoking certain permissions may limit app functionality.

Section 06
Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account:

  • Your personal profile data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Payment and complaint records may be retained for up to 1 year for administrative and legal compliance
  • Anonymized data may be retained for statistical analysis
Section 07
Your Rights

As a user of this app, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications via app Settings or device Settings
  • Withdraw consent by deleting your account at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below or use the Account Deletion feature in your app Settings.

Section 08
Children's Privacy

This app is intended for registered adult residents of Block 13 Federal B Area only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

Section 09
Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date shown at the top of this page.

Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
