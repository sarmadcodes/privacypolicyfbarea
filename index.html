The Block 13 Federal B Area Resident Portal, developed by DeskWorkSolutions, is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a resident.

By registering and using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will never sell or share your data with unauthorized third parties.

Section 01Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you register and use the app:

  • Full name and CNIC (National Identity Card Number)
  • Phone number and email address
  • House number and residential address (Block 13, Federal B Area)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Vehicle registration information, if applicable
  • Payment receipts and transaction IDs
  • Complaint details and communication history
  • Device information and push notification tokens
Section 02How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for community management and resident services:

  • Verifying your identity as a resident of Block 13 Federal B Area
  • Processing and tracking maintenance fee payments
  • Managing and responding to your complaints and requests
  • Sending push notifications for announcements, payment reminders, and complaint updates
  • Issuing your digital resident identity card
  • Maintaining community security records
  • Administrative approval and account management by authorized personnel
Section 03Your Rights

As a user of this app, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications via app Settings or device Settings
  • Withdraw consent by deleting your account at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below or use the Account Deletion feature in your app Settings.

Section 04Children's Privacy

This app is intended for registered adult residents of Block 13 Federal B Area only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

Section 05Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
